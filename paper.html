Abstract:
The stock market is a complex and dynamic system, driven by a number of factors including economic indicators, investor behavior, and global events. To address the problem of the inherent volatility and unpredictability of the stock market, we will apply AI models to predict stock trends, which may potentially offer a more reliable system of prediction. In this paper, we will explore existing research in the field of stock market prediction, outline the team’s approach to the problem, give an overview of the architecture used for our AI model, and present our findings on the effectiveness of our AI model in predicting the stock price of individual stocks.
